Game privacy

The short version: this game stores very little about you.

  • Your progress is saved in this browser, plus an anonymous cloud copy (held with Supabase in the EU, in Stockholm). This is so that if you lose your device, you don't lose your game completely. We do not save your name or other data: just a random player ID.
  • No ads, no tracking, no analytics. Nothing is sold or shared.
  • Donations happen on Donorbox's own site in a new tab. The game never sees your payment details.
  • The share buttons open your own social apps. Nothing from those companies is embedded in this page.
  • The button below deletes your save from this device and from the cloud. There is no undo.
  • This game is not directed at children under 14.
